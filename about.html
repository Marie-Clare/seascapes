Hello!

I'm Marie Sandland

Although I grew up in the middle of England I developed a passion for the sea from a young age. I was happiest on family holidays to Devon where I'd spend two weeks of the year swimming in the sea and playing on the beach. I moved to Dublin in my early twenties and love being close to the sea, usually at Seapoint, which appears in many photos in my portfolio. When I'm not in the sea I'm at home relaxing with my three cats.
